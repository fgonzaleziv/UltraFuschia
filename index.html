In an alternate reality, a kid's biggest dream is to become an astronaut. He applies to a space camp where he gets to go to another planet for a photo op with the president. The ship crash lands before they arrive and the only adult's brain is infested by a parasite that turns him into a merciless killing machine. The kid has to survive and get to the president before the evil chaperone destroys him.
